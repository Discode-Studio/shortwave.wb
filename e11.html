Overview

E11 is a shortwave radio station known for transmitting numeric messages. It is believed to be operated by Polish spy or spy/military forces.

History

The station is associated with variously spy/military communications. E11 transmits sequences of numbers, which have sparked interest and speculation among listeners regarding their meanings.

Functionality

The primary function of E11 is thought to be related to spy/military operations, conveying encrypted messages to personnel. The broadcasts typically feature a clear, mechanical voice delivering sequences of numbers.

Anecdotes

Listeners have documented instances of receiving these numeric broadcasts, often engaging in discussions to decode the messages and understand their significance. The station has fostered a community of amateur radio enthusiasts.

Recent Activity

E11 remains active, with regular broadcasts noted by the radio listening community, continuing to raise discussions regarding its operational significance.
